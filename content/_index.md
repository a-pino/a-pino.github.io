# Alexander Pino

I'm a full-stack software engineer and AI developer currently deploying AI agents in the physical world at [Accenture Industry X](https://www.accenture.com/us-en/services/digital-engineering-manufacturing). As the lead of a five-person moonshot team, I combine the latest developments in knowledge work automation with deep industrial software expertise — building agents for factories, construction sites, mines, and electrical plants that handle fieldwork productivity and site management.

Previously I was a research intern at [FrontierLab@OsakaU](https://www.lif.med.osaka-u.ac.jp/eng/) and a fellow at [WhiteBox Research](https://www.whitebox-research.org/), where I worked on LLM safety benchmarking in collaboration with [Apart Research](https://www.apartresearch.com/).
